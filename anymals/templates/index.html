ABOUT US

Who are we?

LPH
- THE WORLD OF STYLISH DESIGN AND HOME COMFORT FOR ANIMALS -

 

We decided to participate in the junior achievement project and created our own SMU with the name "LPH" (Lovely Pet’s Houses ). This is our first experience, but we are very motivated and want to achieve success. Why animal houses? We don't even know. We just started thinking about what we could do ourselves, what kind of business we could open and came to the conclusion that we both love animals. Then we started thinking how can we distinguish ourselves from the competition?

 

Why this particular design?

 

  • It is stylish, fashionable, modern and suitable for any design of any apartment or private house

 

  • It is eco-friendly, as the houses are made of wood

 

  • You will not find such a design of houses in a regular Zoo store - our houses are competitive

 

  • Each house is filled with its own idea and the design is matched to the functionality of the house

 

 

Why do you need to choose our company LPH?

 

  • The house will fill your home with comfort and beauty

 

  • You will please yourself and your pet

 

  • You will be different from others having such a house, as all houses are special and designer

 

  • We are a start-up business, so you can support us in our endeavors)))))

​

How will we help society if we manage to realize our business and people will buy our houses?

 

 

  • From the remaining materials we will make houses for stray cats and dogs

 

  • So buy our houses and contribute your help to stray cats and dogs!!!
